Our Vision

A society where every woman feels safe, empowered, and respected. We envision an Andhra Pradesh where gender-based violence is eliminated and women can participate fully in all aspects of social, economic, and political life.

{% endif %}

Our Core Values

Protection

Ensuring the safety and security of women through proactive measures and rapid response systems.

Empowerment

Enabling women to become self-reliant and confident through education and skill development programs.

Justice

Ensuring fair treatment and legal support for all women, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Compassion

Providing emotional support and care to women who have experienced trauma or violence.

Collaboration

Working together with communities, organizations, and stakeholders to achieve our goals.

Education

Promoting awareness and education about women's rights, safety measures, and available resources.
